Services

What H-Legacy can help you with.

Whether you’re running a cleaning company, trades business, or another service-based operation, the problems are similar: staff, pricing, and time. We make them visible – and fixable.

01 · Profit Audit
Full business profit & leakage review

We take your real numbers – not guesses – and map out where money is slipping away. Labour, materials, re-work, and pricing are all analysed so you can see exactly what’s costing you.

Delivered as: visual report + action list.
02 · Service & Pricing
Service-level and client-level profitability

Not all jobs are created equal. We break out which services, sites, or clients carry the profit – and which ones quietly drain you – so you can adjust prices or boundaries with confidence.

Ideal for: cleaning, trades, and field-based teams.
03 · Labour & Operations
Staff hours, scheduling & re-work analysis

Deep dive into roster vs actual hours, travel time, overtime, and re-visits. The goal is simple: stop paying extra for jobs that don’t pay you back.

Outcome: clearer targets for staff and supervisors.
How it works

A simple, owner-friendly consulting process.

No big corporate workshops, no ten people in suits. Just a focused review, clear numbers, and a plan you can actually use with your team.

1
Intro call & quick fit check

A short conversation to understand your business, how you currently track jobs and staff, and what “good” would look like for you in the next 12 months.

2
Data intake & review

You’ll send through your existing numbers – even if they’re messy. We work with what you have: accounting exports, basic spreadsheets, scheduling tools, or job systems.

3
H-Legacy Profit Audit

Behind the scenes, your data is run through a purpose-built analysis model to highlight profit leaks, risky clients or services, and quick wins you can act on.

4
Walk-through & action plan

We walk through your report together in plain English. You leave with a prioritised action list, not a pile of theory – and the option for ongoing support if you want it.

What business owners usually want to know upfront:
  • “Will this actually help me raise prices or fix margins?”
  • “Can you explain it in a way my team will understand?”
  • “How quickly will I see the impact of changes?”

Those are exactly the questions we design the Profit Audit around – so you’re not left staring at spreadsheets, you’re making clearer decisions.
