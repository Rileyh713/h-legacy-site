Profitability & margin analysis

Where profit is really made
A structured review of your revenue, direct costs and delivery effort so you can see:
  • Which activities, products or services drive the highest margin.
  • Where revenue looks strong but profit contribution is weak.
  • How discounting, re-work and scope creep impact profitability.

Labour & delivery efficiency

Time vs return
We align workload, labour cost and actual delivery so you can understand:
  • Where teams or functions consistently run over expected effort.
  • How overtime, allowances or last-minute work affect cost-to-serve.
  • Which types of work absorb disproportionate time for limited return.
Output: labour and delivery efficiency dashboard. Ideal for: teams working hard with unclear payoff.

Revenue mix & pricing review

Balance & exposure
We examine your revenue mix and pricing structure so you can:
  • See which lines of work are carrying overall performance.
  • Identify under-priced or structurally low-margin activities.
  • Understand where adjustment or repositioning may protect profit.
Output: revenue mix snapshot and pricing observations. Ideal for: owners considering change or growth.
